More information about Intervarsity

I am involved with Intervarsity (IV). Intervarsity is a Christian student organization on Weber State campus. We meet every Thursdays to study the bible and meet new people from different backgrounds, cultures, and countries. Not only do we study the bible, we also play games and dine as a united group on campus. I am one of the leaders of the group because of my ambition to becoming a better, honest, and role model for my fellow peers.

Over the year my life has been filled with different choices and doubts as to what I really like, but as I paved towards collage, my interest has been narrowed down to certainty. My interest are channeled towards computer programming, softwares, data creation and manipulation. All these are combinations of my hobbies. I really admire the fact that I have the chance to meet new people in my Intervarsity group and hangout with them. During the past years, I’ve realized that meeting new people has been a great blessing towards my spiritual and social life. Speaking about meeting new people, I’ve seen the opportunity to interact with my fellow Christian students. I also have a Bible Study group I lead in the dorms, which is very awesome.
